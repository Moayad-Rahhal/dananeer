Delete Your Account

You can now delete your account directly from the app. Please note that this feature is only available for users who have linked their account to an authentication method (Email, Facebook, Apple ID, etc.). Guests cannot delete their account using this method.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the app and go to the Profile section in the navigation bar.
  2. Tap on Account.
  3. Hit the Delete Account button.
  4. Confirm your action. Once confirmed, your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted.

What data will be deleted?

  • Account information (email, Facebook, Apple ID)
  • Game progress
  • Purchases linked to your account
  • Any personal data stored on our servers

Data Retention

Your account and data will be permanently deleted immediately after you confirm the deletion in the app.
