About Us

Dananeer is building the ultimate home for card and board games—designed for players to enjoy offline and online, with a vision to expand into local play in the future. Our mission is to bring people together through the timeless joy of card and board games, offering flexibility for every play style. Whether you want to compete online with players worldwide or enjoy a game without internet access, Dananeer provides a seamless experience across all modes in one app. We design and develop our games in-house, ensuring quality, cultural authenticity, and innovation with every release. With a growing portfolio of beloved classics and modern experiences, Dananeer is shaping the future of social gaming in the Middle East and beyond. Driven by a passionate team, our vision is to make Dananeer the go-to destination for card and board game lovers everywhere—a community where tradition meets technology, and play never stops.
